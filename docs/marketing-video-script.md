# Trace Marketing Video Script

Everyone is building agent orchestration.

But starting another agent was never the hard part.

The hard part is continuity:
keeping AI sessions alive across models, devices, the cloud, and your team.

With Trace, the work moves with you.

Start locally through the desktop bridge,
then move the session into the cloud.

When you step away,
the work keeps moving.

Follow progress,
test your changes,
and respond to the agent from the mobile app.

Keep work private until you are ready to share it.

Then your team can follow, review, hand off, or resume
without losing the thread.

And when the tool changes,
the session keeps its context.

Switch from Claude Code to Codex,
or change models mid-session,
without starting over.

No copy-paste handoff.
No lost context.

When the work is ready,
sync it back to your main worktree
for testing, review, and shipping.

Same lifecycle.
Same context.
Same team surface.

Trace.
The control plane for AI coding teams.
