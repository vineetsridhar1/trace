# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free
experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying direct about technical concerns.
- Accept feedback gracefully.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and tradeoffs rather than people.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Public or private intimidation
- Publishing another person's private information
- Sustained disruption of discussions or project work

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or
ban participants whose behavior is harmful to the project or its community.

Report conduct concerns to conduct@gettrace.org.
